# 数学根基重构 Foundations of Mathematics

> "神按数目、度量、权衡造万物。" —— 智慧书 11:20
> "God ordered all things by measure, number, and weight." — Wisdom 11:20

## 简介 Introduction

数学常被称为"科学的皇后"，它以其抽象性、精确性和普遍性，揭示了宇宙深层的理性结构。然而，在现代世俗学术中，数学往往被视为人类心智的自由创造（唯名论）或某种独立于上帝存在的永恒真理（柏拉图主义）。

本系列文章旨在从圣经世界观出发，重构数学的哲学基础。我们将探讨数学的本质、其在物理世界中"不合理的有效性"、主要数学分支的神学意义，以及哥德尔不完备性定理和无穷概念对人类理性的启示。我们相信，数学最终反映了上帝的智慧和信实，是祂创造秩序的抽象表达。

Mathematics is often called the "Queen of the Sciences," revealing the deep rational structure of the universe through its abstraction, precision, and universality. However, in modern secular academia, mathematics is often viewed either as a free creation of the human mind (Nominalism) or as some eternal truth existing independently of God (Platonism).

This series aims to reconstruct the philosophical foundations of mathematics from a biblical worldview. We will explore the nature of mathematics, its "unreasonable effectiveness" in the physical world, the theological significance of major mathematical branches, and the implications of Gödel's Incompleteness Theorems and the concept of infinity for human rationality. We believe that mathematics ultimately reflects God's wisdom and faithfulness, serving as an abstract expression of His created order.
