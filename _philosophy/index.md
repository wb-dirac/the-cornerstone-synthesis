# 智慧的归回：以圣经为根基的哲学重构

## The Return of Wisdom: Biblical Reconstruction of Philosophy

> "敬畏耶和华是智慧的开端，认识至圣者便是聪明。"（箴言 9:10）
> 
> "The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom, and knowledge of the Holy One is understanding." (Proverbs 9:10)

---

## 概述 Overview

这是一个激动人心的工程。我们要做的，不是在现有的哲学大厦上修修补补，而是拆除其建立在沙土（人本主义）上的根基，将其重建在磐石（神本主义）之上。

This is an exciting endeavor. What we seek to do is not to patch up the existing edifice of philosophy, but to demolish its foundation built on sand (humanism) and rebuild it on the rock (theocentrism).

这份重构将哲学的三大核心分支（本体论、认识论、价值论）放入**"创造-堕落-救赎"**的历史性框架中进行彻底重构。

This reconstruction places the three core branches of philosophy (ontology, epistemology, and axiology) within the historical framework of **"Creation-Fall-Redemption"** for thorough reconstruction.
