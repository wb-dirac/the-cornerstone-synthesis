# 3.1 伦理学：命令神学 vs. 功利主义
## Ethics: Divine Command vs. Utilitarianism

> "你们要圣洁，因为我是圣洁的。"（利未记 11:44）
>
> "Be holy, because I am holy." (Leviticus 11:44)

---

## 一、世俗伦理学的困境 I. Secular Ethics' Dilemma

### 休谟断头台：事实与价值的鸿沟 Hume's Guillotine: Is-Ought Gap

**"从'是'（Is）无法推导出'应该'（Ought）"**

**"Cannot derive 'Ought' from 'Is'"**

- 描述性陈述（事实）≠ 规范性陈述（价值）
- Descriptive statements (facts) ≠ Normative statements (values)
- 观察自然无法得出道德律
- Observing nature cannot yield moral law

### 功利主义的问题 Problems with Utilitarianism

**原则**："最大多数人的最大幸福"

**Principle**: "Greatest happiness for greatest number"

**问题**：
1. **谁定义幸福**？
   - **Who defines happiness**?
   
2. **可否牺牲少数**？（如活摘器官救五人）
   - **Can sacrifice minority**? (e.g., harvest organs to save five)

3. **后果无法预测**
   - **Consequences unpredictable**

---

## 二、圣经伦理学：神的本性即标准 II. Biblical Ethics: God's Nature as Standard

### 神的性格是道德的源头 God's Character as Source of Morality

**"你们要圣洁，因为我耶和华你们的神是圣洁的。"（利未记 19:2）**

**"Be holy because I, the LORD your God, am holy." (Leviticus 19:2)**

- 善不是上帝任意定的规则
- Good not arbitrary rules God sets
- 善是上帝本性的流露
- Good flows from God's nature

### 动机伦理 Motive Ethics

不仅看行为后果，更看心是否指向荣耀神。

Not only consequences but whether heart aims to glorify God.

**"无论做什么，都要为荣耀神而行。"（哥林多前书 10:31）**

**"So whether you eat or drink or whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God." (1 Corinthians 10:31)**

---

## 三、实践应用 III. Practical Applications

### 绝对道德标准 Absolute Moral Standards

- 谋杀、偷盗、奸淫永远是错的
- Murder, theft, adultery always wrong
- 基于上帝不变的性格
- Based on God's immutable character

### 情境智慧 Situational Wisdom

- 应用原则需要智慧
- Applying principles requires wisdom
- 如大卫吃陈设饼（可 2:25-26）
- Like David eating showbread (Mark 2:25-26)
