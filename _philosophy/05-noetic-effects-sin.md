# 2.2 罪对理性的影响
## Noetic Effects of Sin

> "他们虽然知道神，却不当作神荣耀祂，也不感谢祂。他们的思念变为虚妄，无知的心就昏暗了。"（罗马书 1:21）
>
> "For although they knew God, they neither glorified him as God nor gave thanks to him, but their thinking became futile and their foolish hearts were darkened." (Romans 1:21)

---

## 一、世俗假设：理性是中立的 I. Secular Assumption: Reason is Neutral

### 启蒙运动的神话 Enlightenment Myth

**核心信念**：人的理性是客观、中立、无瑕疵的"法官"

**Core belief**: Human reason is objective, neutral, flawless "judge"

- **笛卡尔**：清楚明白的观念必然真实
- **Descartes**: Clear and distinct ideas are necessarily true

- **康德**：理性可自我立法（道德自律）
- **Kant**: Reason can legislate for itself (moral autonomy)

- **现代科学主义**：科学方法是价值中立的
- **Modern scientism**: Scientific method is value-neutral

### 假设的表现 Manifestations of This Assumption

1. **教育领域**："批判性思维"假设学生能客观判断
   - **Education**: "Critical thinking" assumes students can judge objectively

2. **法律领域**："理性人"假设行为者会理性选择
   - **Law**: "Rational person" assumes actors make rational choices

3. **道德讨论**："理性对话"假设各方能达成共识
   - **Moral discourse**: "Rational dialogue" assumes parties can reach consensus

---

## 二、圣经真理：罪败坏了理性 II. Biblical Truth: Sin Corrupts Reason

### 全然败坏（Total Depravity）不是全然无能 Total Depravity ≠ Total Inability

**改革宗教义**：全然败坏（Total Depravity）

**Reformed doctrine**: Total Depravity

**含义**：
- **非**"人完全丧失理性能力"（人仍能思考、科研）
- **Not** "humans completely lose rational capacity" (can still think, do science)

- **而是**"罪影响了人的每个层面"（理性、情感、意志）
- **But** "sin affects every aspect of humanity" (reason, emotions, will)

### 罪对理性的三重影响 Three Effects of Sin on Reason

#### 1. 压抑真理（Suppression of Truth）

**"原来，神的忿怒从天上显明在一切不虔不义的人身上，就是那些行不义**阻挡真理**的人。"（罗马书 1:18）**

**"The wrath of God is being revealed from heaven against all the godlessness and wickedness of people, who **suppress the truth** by their wickedness." (Romans 1:18)**

**机制**：
- **知道**：人心深处知道上帝存在（罗 1:19-20）
- **Know**: Deep down humans know God exists (Rom 1:19-20)

- **不愿**：不愿承认，因为不想顺服
- **Unwilling**: Unwilling to admit because don't want to submit

- **压抑**：故意压制这知识，用各种理论自欺
- **Suppress**: Deliberately suppress this knowledge, deceiving self with various theories

**例证**：
- 托马斯·内格尔（无神论哲学家）坦承："我不希望上帝存在...这源于恐惧。"
- Thomas Nagel (atheist philosopher) admits: "I don't want there to be a God...It's rooted in fear."

#### 2. 思念虚妄（Futile Thinking）

**"他们的思念变为虚妄...自称为聪明，反成了愚拙。"（罗马书 1:21-22）**

**"Their thinking became futile...Although they claimed to be wise, they became fools." (Romans 1:21-22)**

**表现**：
- **偶像崇拜**："将不能朽坏之神的荣耀变为...受造之物的样式"（罗 1:23）
- **Idolatry**: "Exchanged the glory of the immortal God for images made to look like...mortal man" (Rom 1:23)

- **理论的破碎**：哲学体系内在矛盾（如休谟的怀疑论）
- **Fragmented theories**: Internal contradictions in philosophical systems (like Hume's skepticism)

- **无法整合知识**：学科分裂，失去统一视野
- **Unable to integrate knowledge**: Disciplines fragmented, losing unified vision

#### 3. 心灵昏暗（Darkened Heart）

**"无知的心就昏暗了。"（罗马书 1:21）**

**"Their foolish hearts were darkened." (Romans 1:21)**

**"此等不信之人被这世界的神弄瞎了心眼，不叫基督荣耀福音的光照着他们。"（哥林多后书 4:4）**

**"The god of this age has blinded the minds of unbelievers, so that they cannot see the light of the gospel." (2 Corinthians 4:4)**

**后果**：
- **看不见明显真理**：创造的证据清楚，却视而不见
- **Cannot see obvious truth**: Evidence of creation is clear, yet blind to it

- **称恶为善**：道德颠倒（赛 5:20"祸哉！那些称恶为善，称善为恶的人"）
- **Calling evil good**: Moral inversion (Isa 5:20 "Woe to those who call evil good and good evil")

## 三、无神论不是智力问题，是道德问题 III. Atheism: Not Intellectual but Moral Problem

### 证据充足，意志拒绝 Sufficient Evidence, Willful Rejection

**"愚顽人心里说：'没有神。'"（诗篇 14:1）**

**"The fool says in his heart, 'There is no God.'" (Psalm 14:1)**

**注意**："愚顽"（fool）在圣经中主要是**道德评价**，非智力评价

**Note**: "Fool" in Scripture is primarily **moral assessment**, not intellectual

#### 诗篇14篇的上下文 Context of Psalm 14

- "他们都是邪恶，行了可憎恶的事。"（诗 14:1）
- "They are corrupt, their deeds are vile." (Ps 14:1)

- 无神论与道德败坏相连，不是中立的理性结论
- Atheism linked with moral corruption, not neutral rational conclusion

### 奥尔德斯·赫胥黎的坦白 Aldous Huxley's Confession

**"我有动机不希望世界有意义...因为对我和我的同代人来说，无意义哲学本质上是一种解放工具。**我们反对道德**，因为道德干涉我们的性自由。"**

**"I had motives for not wanting the world to have meaning...For myself, as no doubt for most of my contemporaries, the philosophy of meaninglessness was essentially **an instrument of liberation**. **We objected to morality** because it interfered with our sexual freedom."**

—Aldous Huxley, *Ends and Means* (1937)

### 理性的伪装 Reason as Disguise

**功能**：理性成为为既定意志辩护的工具

**Function**: Reason becomes tool to justify predetermined will

- **不是**：证据 → 结论
- **Not**: Evidence → Conclusion

- **而是**：欲望 → 寻找支持理由
- **But**: Desire → Seeking supporting reasons

**乔纳森·海特的研究**（心理学家）：
- 人先有道德直觉，后用理性辩护
- Humans first have moral intuitions, then use reason to justify

## 四、理性作为工具，非法官 IV. Reason as Instrument, Not Judge

### 正确理解理性的功能 Proper Understanding of Reason's Function

#### 理性 = 眼睛，启示 = 光 Reason = Eye, Revelation = Light

- **眼睛的功能**：接受光，看见事物
- **Eye's function**: Receiving light, seeing things

- **眼睛不是光源**：不能自己产生光
- **Eye is not light source**: Cannot produce light itself

- **若无光**：眼睛再好也看不见
- **Without light**: Even good eyes cannot see

**应用**：
- **理性的功能**：理解、分析、组织信息
- **Reason's function**: Understanding, analyzing, organizing information

- **理性不是真理源头**：需要启示提供内容
- **Reason not truth's source**: Needs revelation to provide content

- **若无启示**：理性陷入黑暗（世俗哲学的困境）
- **Without revelation**: Reason trapped in darkness (secular philosophy's dilemma)

### 范泰尔的比喻：有色眼镜 Van Til's Analogy: Tinted Glasses

**所有人都戴着"世界观眼镜"**：

**Everyone wears "worldview glasses"**:

- **信徒**：圣经的眼镜（虽然仍需清洗）
- **Believers**: Biblical glasses (though still need cleaning)

- **非信徒**：人本主义的眼镜（扭曲一切）
- **Non-believers**: Humanistic glasses (distorting everything)

**没有"中立"观察者**：
- **No "neutral" observers**:
- 每个人都从某个预设出发
- Everyone starts from some presupposition

## 五、罪的认知后果的具体表现 V. Specific Manifestations of Sin's Cognitive Effects

### 1. 逻辑悖论与自相矛盾 Logical Paradoxes and Self-Contradictions

#### 例1：相对主义 Example 1: Relativism

**主张**："没有绝对真理。"

**Claim**: "There is no absolute truth."

**问题**：这句话本身是绝对真理吗？
- 如果是 → 自相矛盾（至少有一个绝对真理）
- 如果不是 → 为何要接受它？

**Problem**: Is this statement itself absolute truth?
- If yes → self-contradictory (at least one absolute truth exists)
- If no → why accept it?

#### 例2：唯物论 Example 2: Materialism

**主张**："只有物质是真实的。"

**Claim**: "Only matter is real."

**问题**：
- 逻辑律（如矛盾律）是物质的吗？
- Are laws of logic (like non-contradiction) material?
- 如果不是，唯物论为何使用逻辑？
- If not, why does materialism use logic?

#### 例3：进化论作为哲学 Example 3: Evolutionism as Philosophy

**主张**："人的思维是进化的产物。"

**Claim**: "Human thinking is product of evolution."

**问题**（C.S. Lewis）：
- 如果思维仅为生存优势而进化，为何相信它能发现真理？
- If thinking evolved merely for survival advantage, why trust it to discover truth?
- 生存≠真理（有时错误信念也有助生存）
- Survival ≠ Truth (sometimes false beliefs aid survival)

### 2. 道德相对主义 Moral Relativism

**主张**："道德只是文化建构。"

**Claim**: "Morality is merely cultural construction."

**问题**：
- 为何谴责纳粹、奴隶制、种族灭绝？
- Why condemn Nazis, slavery, genocide?
- 如果道德相对，没有立场批判其他文化
- If morality is relative, no grounds to critique other cultures

**C.S. Lewis**："当你说某事不公义，你预设了公义的标准。"

**C.S. Lewis**: "When you say something is unjust, you presuppose a standard of justice."

### 3. 虚无主义 Nihilism

**主张**："生命没有意义。"

**Claim**: "Life has no meaning."

**问题**：
- 为何活着？为何不自杀？（加缪的问题）
- Why live? Why not suicide? (Camus's question)
- 为何写书劝说别人生命无意义？（自相矛盾）
- Why write books persuading others life is meaningless? (Self-contradictory)

## 六、对不同领域的影响 VI. Impact on Different Fields

### 1. 科学领域 Science

**罪的影响**：
- 将自然神化（进化论作为替代宗教）
- Deifying nature (evolutionism as substitute religion)
- 拒绝设计的证据（智能设计被排斥）
- Rejecting evidence of design (intelligent design excluded)

### 2. 伦理学 Ethics

**罪的影响**：
- 称恶为善（堕胎、安乐死）
- Calling evil good (abortion, euthanasia)
- 否定绝对道德（一切相对化）
- Denying absolute morality (relativizing everything)

### 3. 历史学 History

**罪的影响**：
- 否定上帝在历史中的作为
- Denying God's acts in history
- 将历史解释为权力斗争（马克思）或偶然（后现代）
- Interpreting history as power struggle (Marx) or chance (postmodern)

### 4. 心理学 Psychology

**罪的影响**：
- 否定罪的概念（弗洛伊德：罪疚感是病态）
- Denying concept of sin (Freud: guilt is pathological)
- 将人还原为欲望的集合
- Reducing humans to bundle of desires

## 七、盼望：救赎更新理性 VII. Hope: Redemption Renews Reason

### 不是绝望的诊断 Not Hopeless Diagnosis

虽然罪败坏了理性，但：

Though sin corrupts reason, yet:

- **普遍恩典**：非信徒仍能做出贡献（艺术、科学）
- **Common grace**: Non-believers still contribute (art, science)

- **良心**：仍有道德意识（罗 2:14-15）
- **Conscience**: Moral consciousness remains (Rom 2:14-15)

- **救赎盼望**：基督更新一切，包括思维
- **Hope of redemption**: Christ renews all, including thinking
