# 序言：哲学的新地基
## Preface: The New Foundation of Philosophy

> "因为经上说：'我要灭绝智慧人的智慧，废弃聪明人的聪明。'智慧人在哪里？文士在哪里？这世上的辩士在哪里？神岂不是叫这世上的智慧变成愚拙吗？"（哥林多前书 1:19-20）
>
> "For it is written: 'I will destroy the wisdom of the wise; the intelligence of the intelligent I will frustrate.' Where is the wise person? Where is the teacher of the law? Where is the philosopher of this age? Has not God made foolish the wisdom of the world?" (1 Corinthians 1:19-20)

## 一、哲学的定义重置 I. Philosophy Redefined

### 传统定义的困境 The Dilemma of Traditional Definitions

在世俗学术传统中，哲学被定义为"爱智慧"（Philo-Sophia），人凭借自主理性探寻真理、善、美的学问。然而，这个看似崇高的事业却始终陷入困境：

In secular academic tradition, philosophy is defined as "love of wisdom" (Philo-Sophia), the discipline where humans seek truth, goodness, and beauty through autonomous reason. However, this seemingly noble endeavor has always been trapped in predicaments:

- **怀疑论的深渊**：从苏格拉底的"我知道我一无所知"到休谟对因果律的怀疑，理性主义者越追求确定性，越陷入不可知论。
- **The Abyss of Skepticism**: From Socrates' "I know that I know nothing" to Hume's doubt about causality, the more rationalists pursue certainty, the deeper they sink into agnosticism.

- **相对主义的泛滥**：后现代哲学宣告"没有绝对真理"，将一切价值判断归为权力话语和文化建构。
- **The Flood of Relativism**: Postmodern philosophy declares "there is no absolute truth," reducing all value judgments to power discourse and cultural construction.

- **虚无主义的终局**：尼采宣告"上帝已死"之后，人类失去了意义的根基，只剩下权力意志的赤裸挣扎。
- **The End of Nihilism**: After Nietzsche's declaration that "God is dead," humanity lost the foundation of meaning, left only with the naked struggle of the will to power.

### 圣经的重新定义 Biblical Redefinition

圣经提供了哲学的真正定义和方向：

Scripture provides the true definition and direction of philosophy:

**"基督...在祂里面藏着一切智慧知识的宝藏。"（歌罗西书 2:3）**

**"Christ, in whom are hidden all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge." (Colossians 2:3)**

哲学不再是人凭己意寻找真理的徒劳，而是：
- **认识基督**：祂本身就是神的智慧（林前 1:24）
- **在基督里理解万有**：因为万有都是靠祂造的，又是为祂造的（西 1:16）
- **将心意夺回**：使一切思想都顺服基督（林后 10:5）

Philosophy is no longer the futile human attempt to find truth by one's own will, but:
- **Knowing Christ**: He Himself is the wisdom of God (1 Cor 1:24)
- **Understanding all things in Christ**: For all things were created by Him and for Him (Col 1:16)
- **Taking captive every thought**: Making all thinking obedient to Christ (2 Cor 10:5)

## 二、核心矛盾：自主理性 vs. 依靠启示 II. Central Conflict: Autonomous Reason vs. Relational Revelation

### 自主理性的僭越 The Usurpation of Autonomous Reason

自启蒙运动以来，西方哲学的核心信念是：**人的理性可以独立于上帝，靠自身达到真理**。

Since the Enlightenment, the core belief of Western philosophy has been: **human reason can independently reach truth apart from God**.

这种信念体现在：

This belief manifests in:

1. **笛卡尔的"我思故我在"**：将怀疑的自我设为哲学起点
   - **Descartes' "I think, therefore I am"**: Setting the doubting self as the starting point of philosophy

2. **康德的"理性批判"**：试图划定理性的界限，却将上帝置于"不可知"之墙外
   - **Kant's "Critique of Reason"**: Attempting to delimit reason's boundaries while placing God beyond the wall of the "unknowable"

3. **尼采的"重估一切价值"**：人成为价值的创造者，取代上帝的位置
   - **Nietzsche's "Revaluation of All Values"**: Man becomes the creator of values, replacing God's position

### 启示的优先性 The Priority of Revelation

圣经揭示了一个根本不同的认识论基础：

Scripture reveals a fundamentally different epistemological foundation:

**"敬畏耶和华是知识的开端；愚妄人藐视智慧和训诲。"（箴言 1:7）**

**"The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge, but fools despise wisdom and instruction." (Proverbs 1:7)**

真正的知识不是从怀疑的自我开始，而是从：
- **谦卑接受启示**：上帝主动向人说话
- **关系性真理**：真理不是抽象命题，而是位格性的上帝自我启示
- **信心与理性的统一**：信心不是反理性，而是理性得以正常运作的前提

True knowledge does not begin with the doubting self, but from:
- **Humble reception of revelation**: God actively speaks to humanity
- **Relational truth**: Truth is not abstract propositions but God's personal self-revelation
- **Unity of faith and reason**: Faith is not anti-rational but the prerequisite for reason to function properly

## 三、重构总原则 III. General Principles of Reconstruction

### 1. 创造 Creation：万有原本的秩序与关联

**"起初，神创造天地。"（创世记 1:1）**

在创造中，我们看到：
- **理性的基础**：宇宙有秩序、可理解，因为是理性的上帝创造的
- **The Foundation of Rationality**: The universe is ordered and comprehensible because it was created by a rational God

- **真理的统一**：一切真理最终归于一（三位一体的上帝），因此哲学的各分支本质上相互关联
- **Unity of Truth**: All truth ultimately converges in the One (Triune God), thus all branches of philosophy are essentially interconnected

- **目的性**：受造界不是偶然的，而是有目的、有意义的
- **Purposefulness**: Creation is not accidental but purposeful and meaningful

### 2. 堕落 Fall：人类理性的昏暗与哲学的破碎

**"他们虽然知道神，却不当作神荣耀祂，也不感谢祂。他们的思念变为虚妄，无知的心就昏暗了。"（罗马书 1:21）**

堕落带来的认知后果：

Cognitive consequences of the Fall:

- **理性的扭曲**：人的理性仍能运作，但方向错误，总是远离真理
- **Distorted Reason**: Human reason still functions, but in the wrong direction, always moving away from truth

- **知识的破碎**：哲学分支（本体论、认识论、价值论）被割裂，无法形成统一体系
- **Fragmented Knowledge**: Branches of philosophy (ontology, epistemology, axiology) are severed, unable to form a unified system

- **压抑真理**：不是缺乏证据，而是故意压抑不喜欢的真理（罗 1:18）
- **Suppressing Truth**: Not lacking evidence but deliberately suppressing unwelcome truth (Rom 1:18)

### 3. 救赎 Redemption：在基督里，万有重新同归于一

**"要照所安排的，在日期满足的时候，使天上地上一切所有的，都在基督里面同归于一。"（以弗所书 1:10）**

救赎不仅拯救人的灵魂，也更新人的思想：

Redemption not only saves the soul but renews the mind:

- **更新的心志**："不要效法这个世界，只要心意更新而变化"（罗 12:2）
- **Renewed Mind**: "Do not conform to the pattern of this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind" (Rom 12:2)

- **在基督里重新整合**：破碎的知识体系在基督里找到统一
- **Reintegration in Christ**: Fragmented knowledge systems find unity in Christ

- **光照与理解**："我们...所领受的，乃是从神来的灵，叫我们能知道神开恩赐给我们的事"（林前 2:12）
- **Illumination and Understanding**: "We have received...the Spirit who is from God, so that we may understand what God has freely given us" (1 Cor 2:12)

## 四、重构的路径与方法 IV. Path and Method of Reconstruction

### 批判性分析 Critical Analysis

我们不是简单地拒绝世俗哲学，而是：

We do not simply reject secular philosophy, but:

1. **识别预设**：揭示每个哲学体系背后的世界观预设
   - **Identify presuppositions**: Reveal the worldview assumptions behind each philosophical system

2. **内在批判**：展示世俗哲学体系的内在矛盾和不足
   - **Internal critique**: Demonstrate internal contradictions and inadequacies of secular philosophical systems

3. **归谬法**：将世俗预设推到逻辑终点，显明其荒谬性
   - **Reductio ad absurdum**: Push secular presuppositions to their logical conclusions to reveal their absurdity

### 积极重建 Positive Reconstruction

在批判的基础上，我们积极重建：

Building on critique, we positively reconstruct:

1. **从圣经出发**：以圣经启示为认识论基础
   - **Starting from Scripture**: Taking biblical revelation as epistemological foundation

2. **整全视角**：在创造-堕落-救赎框架中理解哲学问题
   - **Holistic perspective**: Understanding philosophical problems within the Creation-Fall-Redemption framework

3. **实践导向**：哲学不仅是抽象思辨，更要服侍上帝国度和人类繁荣
   - **Practice-oriented**: Philosophy is not merely abstract speculation but serves God's kingdom and human flourishing

这不仅是一项学术工作，更是一次灵性之旅：

This is not merely an academic endeavor but a spiritual journey:

- **谦卑求索**：承认人的有限性，依靠上帝的启示
- **Humble inquiry**: Acknowledging human limitation and relying on God's revelation

- **勇敢批判**：不惧怕挑战流行观念，忠于真理
- **Courageous critique**: Unafraid to challenge popular notions, faithful to truth

- **敬虔思考**：让哲学思考成为敬拜的一部分
- **Devout thinking**: Making philosophical reflection part of worship

愿这次重构之旅，帮助我们更深地认识那位"太初就有"的道（约 1:1），在祂里面找到真正的智慧。

May this journey of reconstruction help us know more deeply the Word "who was in the beginning" (John 1:1), finding true wisdom in Him.
