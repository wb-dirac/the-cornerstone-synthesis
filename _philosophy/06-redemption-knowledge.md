# 2.3 救赎与知识的恢复
## Redemption and the Restoration of Knowledge

> "你们必晓得真理，真理必叫你们得以自由。"（约翰福音 8:32）
>
> "Then you will know the truth, and the truth will set you free." (John 8:32)

---

## 一、信仰寻求理解 I. Faith Seeking Understanding

### 奥古斯丁的原则 Augustine's Principle

**"Credo ut intelligam"** — "我信，为要理解"

**"Credo ut intelligam"** — "I believe in order that I may understand"

**次序**：
1. **先信**（Faith）：接受上帝的启示
2. **后知**（Understanding）：理性深入理解启示

**Order**:
1. **First believe** (Faith): Accept God's revelation
2. **Then understand** (Understanding): Reason deeply comprehends revelation

**"除非你相信，你不会理解。"（以赛亚书 7:9 七十士译本）**

**"Unless you believe, you will not understand." (Isaiah 7:9 LXX)**

### 安瑟伦的神学方法 Anselm's Theological Method

**"信仰寻求理解"（Fides quaerens intellectum）**

**"Faith seeking understanding" (Fides quaerens intellectum)**

- 信仰不是反理性，而是理性的前提
- Faith not anti-rational, but reason's prerequisite
- 理解是信仰的自然延伸
- Understanding is faith's natural extension

---

## 二、圣灵的光照 II. Illumination of the Holy Spirit

### 重生：属灵眼睛的开启 Regeneration: Opening Spiritual Eyes

**"若有人在基督里，他就是新造的人，旧事已过，都变成新的了。"（哥林多后书 5:17）**

**"Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!" (2 Corinthians 5:17)**

#### 认知的转变 Cognitive Transformation

- **从昏暗到光明**："那吩咐光从黑暗里照出来的神，已经照在我们心里"（林后 4:6）
- **From darkness to light**: "God...made his light shine in our hearts" (2 Cor 4:6)

- **从压抑到接受**：不再压抑真理，而是欢迎真理
- **From suppression to reception**: No longer suppressing but welcoming truth

### 更新的心志 Renewed Mind

**"不要效法这个世界，只要心意更新而变化。"（罗马书 12:2）**

**"Do not conform to the pattern of this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind." (Romans 12:2)**

#### 持续的过程 Ongoing Process

- **称义**（Justification）：瞬间完成
- **Justification**: Instantaneous

- **成圣**（Sanctification）：渐进过程，包括思维的更新
- **Sanctification**: Progressive process, including mind's renewal

---

## 三、真理的恢复 III. Restoration of Truth

### 知识的层次 Levels of Knowledge

#### 1. 救赎性知识 Saving Knowledge

**"认识你独一的真神，并且认识你所差来的耶稣基督，这就是永生。"（约翰福音 17:3）**

**"Now this is eternal life: that they know you, the only true God, and Jesus Christ, whom you have sent." (John 17:3)**

- 最核心的知识：认识上帝
- Most central knowledge: Knowing God
- 关系性的（personal），非仅命题性的
- Relational (personal), not merely propositional

#### 2. 世界观知识 Worldview Knowledge

- 通过圣经眼镜看世界
- Seeing world through biblical glasses
- 理解创造-堕落-救赎框架
- Understanding Creation-Fall-Redemption framework

#### 3. 专业知识 Specialized Knowledge

- 在圣经框架下的科学、哲学、艺术
- Science, philosophy, art within biblical framework
- 文化使命的实践
- Practicing cultural mandate

### 在基督里的整合 Integration in Christ

**"使天上地上一切所有的，都在基督里面同归于一。"（以弗所书 1:10）**

**"To bring unity to all things in heaven and on earth under Christ." (Ephesians 1:10)**

---

## 结论 Conclusion

救赎不仅拯救灵魂，也更新思想，恢复真知识的可能性。

Redemption not only saves souls but renews minds, restoring possibility of true knowledge.
