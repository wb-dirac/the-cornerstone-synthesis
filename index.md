# 基石综合 The Cornerstone Synthesis

## 愿景 Vision

一个宏伟的学术倡议，旨在**以圣经的世界观和救赎历史的元叙事为基石**，系统地重构和整合所有传统学科的基础理论和知识体系，使一切知识在基督里获得其真正的意义和统一性。

A grand academic initiative aimed at systematically **reconstructing and integrating the foundational theories and knowledge systems of all traditional disciplines**, based on the biblical worldview and the meta-narrative of redemption history, so that all knowledge gains its true meaning and unity in Christ.

## 核心原则 Core Principles

### 1. 圣经世界观 Biblical Worldview

圣经不仅是关于救赎的启示，更是关于整个实在的启示。上帝的话语为我们提供了理解宇宙、人类和一切知识的根基。

The Bible is not only a revelation about redemption, but a revelation about all of reality. God's Word provides us with the foundation for understanding the universe, humanity, and all knowledge.

### 2. 救赎历史元叙事 Redemption History Meta-Narrative

创造-堕落-救赎-成全的救赎历史框架为所有知识提供了统一的叙事结构。

The Creation-Fall-Redemption-Consummation framework of redemption history provides a unified narrative structure for all knowledge.

### 3. 基督中心 Christ-Centered

"因为万有都是靠他造的...一切都是藉着他造的，又是为他造的。"（歌罗西书1:16）

"For by him all things were created... all things were created through him and for him." (Colossians 1:16)
