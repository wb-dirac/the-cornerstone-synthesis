# 神学基础 Theological Foundations

## 概述 Overview

本部分阐述支撑整个"基石合成"项目的神学基础。这些基础不是外加于学术研究的宗教教条，而是揭示实在本身的真实结构，为一切知识提供统一的框架。

This section articulates the theological foundations that support the entire "Cornerstone Synthesis" project. These foundations are not religious dogmas imposed on academic research, but reveal the true structure of reality itself, providing a unified framework for all knowledge.

The foundational framework for understanding and interpreting all of reality. Explores:
- God's sovereignty

- Human calling

统一的历史框架：创造-堕落-救赎-成全。阐明：
- 四个历史阶段
- 整合知识的框架
- 各学科的历史定位

The unified historical framework: Creation-Fall-Redemption-Consummation. Illuminates:
- Four historical epochs
- Framework for integrating knowledge
- Historical positioning of disciplines

The nature, sources, and reliability of knowledge. Explores:

宇宙万有的起源、本质和目的。阐明：

The origin, nature, and purpose of all things. Illuminates:
- Characteristics of creation

## 整合原则 Integrative Principles

这些神学基础共同提供：

These theological foundations together provide:

### 1. 统一的元叙事 Unified Meta-Narrative

所有知识和经验都在救赎历史的宏大叙事中获得意义和连贯性。

All knowledge and experience gain meaning and coherence within the grand narrative of redemption history.

### 2. 坚实的认识论 Solid Epistemology

神学认识论为知识的获取和检验提供可靠的基础，避免怀疑主义和相对主义。

Theological epistemology provides a reliable foundation for acquiring and verifying knowledge, avoiding skepticism and relativism.

### 3. 创造的框架 Creational Framework

创造论揭示宇宙的理性结构和目的性，为科学研究和文化发展提供方向。

The doctrine of creation reveals the rational structure and purposefulness of the universe, providing direction for scientific research and cultural development.

### 4. 批判性工具 Critical Tools

圣经世界观提供评估和重构各学科理论的标准和方法。

The biblical worldview provides standards and methods for evaluating and reconstructing disciplinary theories.
