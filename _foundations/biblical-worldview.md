# 圣经世界观 Biblical Worldview

## 引言 Introduction

圣经世界观是理解和诠释一切实在的根基框架。它不仅涉及宗教信仰，更是关于如何认识上帝、宇宙、人类和知识本身的全备视角。

The biblical worldview is the foundational framework for understanding and interpreting all of reality. It is not merely about religious belief, but a comprehensive perspective on how to know God, the universe, humanity, and knowledge itself.

## 核心要素 Core Elements

### 1. 上帝的主权 God's Sovereignty

**"耶和华在天上立定宝座，他的国统管万有。"（诗篇 103:19）**

上帝是宇宙的创造者、维护者和统治者。祂的主权贯穿于：
- 自然界的运行
- 历史的进程
- 人类的生活
- 一切知识的领域

God is the Creator, Sustainer, and Ruler of the universe. His sovereignty encompasses:
- The operation of nature
- The course of history
- Human life
- All domains of knowledge

### 2. 圣经的权威 Biblical Authority

**"圣经都是神所默示的，于教训、督责、使人归正、教导人学义都是有益的。"（提摩太后书 3:16）**

圣经是上帝无误的启示，为我们提供：
- 真理的绝对标准
- 认识实在的基础
- 伦理道德的准则
- 一切知识的解释框架

Scripture is God's inerrant revelation, providing us with:
- The absolute standard of truth
- The foundation for knowing reality
- Ethical and moral norms
- The interpretive framework for all knowledge

### 3. 创造秩序 Created Order

**"自从造天地以来，神的永能和神性是明明可知的，虽是眼不能见，但藉着所造之物就可以晓得。"（罗马书 1:20）**

宇宙是上帝有目的的创造，具有：
- 内在的理性结构
- 可认识的规律
- 目的性的设计
- 善的本质

The universe is God's purposeful creation, characterized by:
- Intrinsic rational structure
- Knowable laws
- Purposeful design
- Essential goodness

### 4. 人的使命 Human Calling

**"神就照着自己的形象造人...神就赐福给他们，又对他们说：'要生养众多，遍满地面，治理这地。'"（创世记 1:27-28）**

人类作为上帝形象的承载者，受托：
- 探索和理解受造界
- 开发和管理资源
- 发展文化和文明
- 荣耀造物主

Humanity, as bearers of God's image, is entrusted to:
- Explore and understand creation
- Develop and steward resources
- Cultivate culture and civilization
- Glorify the Creator

## 对知识的影响 Implications for Knowledge

### 认识论基础 Epistemological Foundation

1. **启示先于理性** - Revelation precedes reason
   - 上帝的自我启示是一切真知识的起点
   - God's self-revelation is the starting point of all true knowledge

2. **信心与理解** - Faith and understanding
   - "信就是所望之事的实底，是未见之事的确据"（希伯来书 11:1）
   - Faith provides the foundation for rational inquiry

3. **整全性** - Comprehensive scope
   - 圣经世界观涵盖生活的每个领域
   - The biblical worldview encompasses every sphere of life

### 真理的统一性 Unity of Truth

**"在你那里有生命的源头，在你的光中，我们必得见光。"（诗篇 36:9）**

所有真理都源于上帝：
- 自然启示与特殊启示的和谐
- 不同学科知识的统一
- 理论与实践的整合

All truth originates from God:
- Harmony between general and special revelation
- Unity of knowledge across disciplines
- Integration of theory and practice

## 应用原则 Application Principles

### 在学术研究中 In Academic Research

1. **预设的检验** - Examining presuppositions
   - 识别和评估各学科的基本假设
   - Identify and evaluate fundamental assumptions in each discipline

2. **重构框架** - Reconstructing frameworks
   - 从圣经原则出发建立理论基础
   - Build theoretical foundations from biblical principles

3. **批判性整合** - Critical integration
   - 辨别真理与错误，保留合宜的知识
   - Discern truth from error, retain appropriate knowledge

### 在实践应用中 In Practical Application

1. **目的导向** - Purposeful orientation
   - 一切学术活动以荣耀上帝为目的
   - All academic activities aim to glorify God

2. **服侍取向** - Service orientation
   - 知识用于服侍他人和社会
   - Knowledge is used to serve others and society

3. **责任意识** - Stewardship consciousness
   - 对所获知识负责任地使用
   - Responsible use of acquired knowledge

## 结论 Conclusion

圣经世界观不是将信仰强加于科学，而是认识到所有知识都有其预设和框架。它邀请我们在上帝的光中重新审视一切知识，使其获得真正的意义和统一性。

The biblical worldview is not about imposing faith on science, but recognizing that all knowledge has its presuppositions and frameworks. It invites us to re-examine all knowledge in the light of God, enabling it to gain true meaning and unity.
